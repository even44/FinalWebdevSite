Welcome to our page!

We are a student group at Oslo Metropolitan university who specializes in inclusive web-design. With this website we seek to give you insight into our life as first year students. We will also give you some information about the different subjects we learn about. This includes web-accessibility, technology’s impact on society and lastly a fun-page for you to enjoy.

You will be able to navigate through our website via our navigation bar at the top of the page. We have included five pages; Home, Reflection, Accessibility, Technology’s impact and the fun-page. Feel free to explore our page as you please!
